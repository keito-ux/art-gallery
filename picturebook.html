Coming Soon: The Star and the Garden

A magical journey into a world of light, kindness, and co-creation

"One of the important goals of this picture book project is to create art that brings beauty, kindness, and happiness to people."

The Story

The story follows a young boy who, one quiet night, discovers a star fairy glowing gently in his room. Curious and enchanted, he follows the fairy through the darkness, eventually stepping into a magical world.

Guided by a gentle reindeer, the boy embarks on a dreamlike journey through this world. Along the way, he plants a mysterious seed and begins to grow a garden — one that slowly blooms with the light and memories he gathers.

Chasing the star symbolizes the pursuit of one's destiny — the longing to follow what truly calls to the heart.

Growing a garden reflects a quiet truth: even though you can't catch a butterfly by chasing it, when you tend to the garden, the butterfly comes to you.

Behind the Scenes

This book is co-created with artists from Ukraine and Japan, bringing together voices across borders. It is a shared space of imagination and expression, where art becomes a bridge of hope.
